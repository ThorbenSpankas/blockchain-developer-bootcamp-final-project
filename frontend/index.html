Gambling Problems

Create Custom ETH Options and bet on future Price developments. For placing a PriceGuess, you need to enter 12 digits. 111100000000 would refer to ethereum Price of 1111.0 USD. The player putting the bet is betting on a price increase, while the taker takes the counter bet. After a bet is placed there is 1 min to take the bet. 2-3 minutes later the bet can be resolved. If a bet is not taken or not resolved, the price pool becomes part of the next betting round.
